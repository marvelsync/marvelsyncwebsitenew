Our Claims Quality Audit Management Tool streamlines audit workflows, enhances claims accuracy, and minimizes compliance risks, helping organizations improve financial performance and operational efficiency.

Key Capabilities:
  • Automated Claims Auditing : Leverage AI-driven and rule-based auditing to detect errors, inconsistencies, and compliance gaps in claims processing.
  • Regulatory Compliance & Standards Adherence : Ensure claims align with CMS, HIPAA, and industry guidelines, reducing the risk of penalties and disputes.
  • Error & Discrepancy Identification : Flag duplicate claims, coding errors, and payment discrepancies in real time to prevent financial losses.
  • Pre-Payment & Post-Payment Audits Conduct proactive audits before claim payment and retrospective reviews to validate accuracy and detect fraud.

  • Root Cause Analysis & Corrective Actions : Identify systemic issues, implement corrective measures, and prevent recurring errors with structured CAPA workflows.
  • Real-Time Dashboards & Reporting : Gain actionable insights through interactive dashboards, audit reports, and trend analysis to enhance decision-making.
  • Integration with Claims Management Systems : Seamlessly connect with existing CMS platforms for a unified and automated claims quality audit process.
